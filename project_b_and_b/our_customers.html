Frequently Asked Questions

How far from Dublin Airport is Moore's B&B?

Great question! Moore's B&B is approximately 120 miles (169km) from Dublin Airport. if you are driving, follow the signs for Galway and Galway City in particular, we are in the heart of Galway City and have free parking for our customers. If taking the bus, goto Dublin's central bus station (Busaras) and take a bus to Galway city. We are 5 minutes walk away from the main city stop

When can I check in?

Great news! At Moore's B&B, we can book you in any time of the day you wish on your arrival day once you state your desired checkin time you wish to checkin by on the day of your arrival. Please note we do not check in customers between midnight and 6am GMT to respect our customer's need for sleep and privacy in those dark hours. Our normal checkin time is between 6am and 6pm to allow housekeeping to prepare your room for your arrival.

Can I cancel my booking and get a refund?

Of course, we understand your plans change and will offer a full refund upto 48 hours before midnight of the day you have booked in as arriving. We will offer a 75% refund within that 48 hour window for cancellations. Rescheduling of bookings is indeed possible when you talk to the team on the phone numbers listed on our website. Please note some rescheduled bookings may incurr a premium fee for the service if rescheduled within 48 hour of the original arrival date.
