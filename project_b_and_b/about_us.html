Our Story

Over twenty years ago, Gillian Moore decided to launch a bread and breafast with a difference. Her view of a quality experience for even just one nights rest meant anybody who booked with her would be remember the kind of stay that would make them come back again and again.

Gillian and her team transformed her qualitative vision into a reality, which was recognised at the Trip Advisor Awards 2016 with a Certificate of Excellence in the Bed and Breakfast catagory. Many of the vistors who stay with her today are professionals or vacationing families that always book with Moore's B&B first because they know they are gauranteed quality at a price they can afford. This makes a return visit to Moore's B&B their first choice anytime they travel to Galway City and County.
